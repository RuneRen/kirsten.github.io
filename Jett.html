Cloudburst (C)

Throw out a cloud of fog that obscures vision on impact. Hold down the ability button to bend the cloud's in-flight trajectory. Pressing C allows Jett to throw out a cloud of fog. You can use this twice per round if you buy the ability for 200creds.

Updraft (Q)

After a brief windup, propel yourself upwards at double and a half an agents height. You can use it once per round if you buy it for 150creds.

Tailwind (E)

This is Jett's signature ability. Immediately dash a short distance in the direction you're moving. If you jump before dashing you can have more distance. You can dash once and can dash again after getting two kills. It is free every round

Blade Storm (X)

Deadly throwing knives that deal 50 damage and kill on headshots. You can either throw a single dagger or all of them. Scoring a kill restores all daggers. You get this ultimate after gathering 8 points from either kills, deaths, or orbs.
