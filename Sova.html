Owl Drone (C)

Sova can deploy and control the movement of the drone which is useful for gathering info on where the enemies are. He can shoot a marking dart with the drone to reveal the location of the enemy player that was struck by the dart. While controlling it, sova can't move. The drone can be destryed by the enemy. You can use it once per round if you buy it for 400cred.

Shock Dart (Q)

Sova uses his bow to shoot a shock bolt. You can control how much power you want to apply to it and choose if you want to bounce it off twice on surfaces or not. You can use it twice per round after buying it for 150creds.

Recon Bolt (E)

This is Sova's signature ability. When Sova shoots the recon it can scan the area three times and reveal the location of the enemy team if they are within the area. You can choose the power and if you want it to bounce off walls, but only twice. The recon can be destroyed by the enemies. You can use it for free but has a cooldown of 40 seconds.

Hunter's Fury (X)

Sova shoots an energy blast that can go through anything, even walls. When an enemy gets hit by the ultimate, their locations get revealed. You can use it three times within 6 seconds. You get this ultimate after gathering 8 points from either kills, deaths, or orbs.
