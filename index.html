WELCOME TO YOUR FRIENDLY GUIDE FOR VALORANT

Valorant is a free-to-play multiplayer first-person shooter (FPS) game developed and published by Riot Games, the same company behind League of Legends. It was officially released on June 2, 2020, for Microsoft Windows. If you're familiar with CSGO or Overwatch then adapting to Valorant will be easier for you since it is a mix of both.

Available On:

  • PC
  • Xbox Series X|S
  • PS5
